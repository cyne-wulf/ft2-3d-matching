# Deal Matching Guide

When the participant says `Run the 3D matching workshop.`, walk through the checkpoints in order:

1. `pi-check`
2. `download-data --dry-run`
3. `verify-data`
4. `prepare-sample`
5. `inspect-record`
6. `textify-record`
7. `vectorize-sample`
8. `start-vector-store`
9. `index-records`
10. `query-vector-store`
11. `compare-vectors`
12. `score-market-stage`
13. `rank-candidates`
14. `evaluate-leads`
15. `setup-airtable`
16. `verify-airtable`
17. `sync-airtable`
18. `read-airtable-labels`
19. `compute-metrics`
20. `render-review-workspace`

For each checkpoint, state the command, what output should appear, what verification passed, and why the step matters. Keep the participant focused on how raw structured records become searchable semantic records, scored leads, reviewed Airtable rows, and measurable outcomes.
